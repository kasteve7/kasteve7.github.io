Game Jam Entries

A game jam is a competition in which individuals or teams create a full video game within an alotted amount of time. This means all parts from the programming to the art assets are developed in the time frame. The amount of time depends on the jam and could be as short as a day or as long as a month. After the development period, the games are typically rated and a winner or winners are announced. I love to participate in these competitions as a sole developer, and I take pride in my projects.
